Our Policy

Check-In/Check-Out

  • Check-in time is 3:00 PM, check-out is 10:00 AM.
  • Early check-in/late check-out subject to availability and fee.
  • ID and credit card required at check-in.

Occupancy Limits

  • Maximum occupancy enforced for safety.
  • Extra charges or additional room for extra guests.

Smoking Policy

  • 100% non-smoking rooms, $250 cleaning fee for violations.
  • Designated smoking areas available.

Payment

  • Room charges and taxes paid at check-in.
  • Accepts Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and cash.

Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellations must be made 24 hours prior to arrival.
  • No-shows and early departures charged for the full stay.

Food Refund Policy

  • All paid food items are non-refundable.
